#+title: Personal Journal

Episteme provides a private =personal journal= for you to utilize.

You can open it using the =main menu=:

- =j= to open the journal for the current day
- =J= to add a new entry for the day

The journal is organized under =episteme/journal= (=~/.config/episteme/journal= by default) with a file-per-week, entry-per-day.
